Fort Collins is nestled against the foothills of the Rocky Mountains and alongside the banks of the Cache la Poudre River. The city lies approximately 5,000 feet above sea level. Residents enjoy a moderate, four season climate with an average of 300 days of sunshine and 14.5 inches of precipitation a year.
